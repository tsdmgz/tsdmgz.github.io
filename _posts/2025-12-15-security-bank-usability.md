Security Bank Philippines is a bank. There's the usual security bits. Their
:sparkles: brand new app :sparkles: is so secure it's unusable!

1. Refuses to run on Android phones with Developer Mode (not even only ADB)
   enabled.
2. Locked me out of my account after only *two* failed OTP attempts, where their
   web form automatically submits the code when you fill out all six digits. God
   help you if you make two typos in a row for the last digit.

Other major legacy banks do similar stupid things. I don't know which bank to go
anymore. Then there are banks I absolutely will not patronize, even with a
ten foot pole.
